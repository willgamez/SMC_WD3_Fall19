Will's coffee

est 2010

Undercaffinated Will realized there was an inherent need to provide his long study sessions with an economical alternative to starbucks

Armed with the internet and a poorly stocked store a block a way, will set out to create a decent cup of joe.

reddit provided the information neccessary to synthesize all the neccesary ingredients to keep him caffinated. Soon Will was making a very respectable cup of coffee, and quickly graduated to a chemex coffee maker and a goose neck kettle to elevate his craft

The aromas of his handiwork would attract quite the following, a need was found and more coffee was made. The rest is history
