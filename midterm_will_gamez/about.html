It started with a group of friends... broke friends

Everybody starts somewhere, here at prime number services our roots were firmly planted by a group of 4 friends. Growing up in L.A. we felt like we were up against other photographers who had an arsenal of gear. We combined our money and each bought a different lens while sharing the same camera body.

PlaceholderUs circa 1995

We each mastered how to shoot our lens then circled back and taught each other

We quickly learned the value of communal learning in the creative process. Coming from different backgrounds, some of us artistic, some of us blue collar, we realized that photography meant different things to each of us. We all agreed that photography should have an easier entry point for the complete novice.

PlaceholderOur first setup

We got better, and still noticed one thing. No matter our set up, the prime lens was still king.

Faster, better quality glass,and better subject seperation. For some forms of photography, the prime lens was not only the economical choice,it was also the best artistic choice. Instead of carrying around a bag full of lenses, knowing your style and showing up with the right gear for the job can save time and money.
